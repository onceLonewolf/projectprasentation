theme 1: Old schoolhaus

The town fire in 1536 destroyed the house down to its foundations. After they rebuild it in 1565, the Latin school and the German school were accommodated there. Hence the traditional name "Old Schoolhouse". The building underwent a thorough restoration between 1975 and 1979.
