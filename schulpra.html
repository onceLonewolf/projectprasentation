Old schoolhaus

The town fire in 1536 destroyed the house down to its foundations. Rebuild in 1567, the Latin school was accommodated there. Hence the descriptive name "Old Schoolhouse". The building underwent a thorough restoration between the years of 1975 and 1979.

An old drawing of Weiden i. d. Opf

The Picture was taken in 1870 and depicts Weiden during it´s industrial revolution ,it depicts luxourious villas and magnificent gardens.

A painting of Max Reger

This picture shows Max Reger, a famous compouser. He was born in Weiden and lived there from 1873 to 1916 the painting was painted in the same year he met his end. Tristen Braun is listed as it´s painter.

Old hunting clothes

The old hunting uniform consisted of a blue coat paired with a blue hat with a traditional wildness icon embeded pn the front. This particular uniform was worn in 1860 by a captain of the hunting associtation.

Military- and Culture museum

This part we will focus on the museum. The museum was opened in 1932 during the second world war and was renovated and restocked in 2015 to expand more on the German-American friendship and the military training area.The building was once usead as a meeting place of the Hitler Youth in grafenwöhr.

Vulkanerlebnis Parkstein

The Vulkan museum was first opened on the 7th of Juli 2013 and servers the education about the basalt cone and how the city Parkstein came to be.

The basalt cone

A basalt cone was taken from the basalt wall that developed around 24 to 22 million jears ago, to be displayed in the museum.

The Volcano eruption (vEent)

If you are lucky or just wait a bit it is possible for the museum to start rumbeling. Not to worry! It is merely an event emulating how a vulcano erruption works small scale. Underneath an iron grid vapor begins to appear, painted red by lights. The speakers start playing loud noises that make your body shiver.

The bell

This bell was used to alarm the people of a big anoucement. It was rung in the city center by the president and was used in the 1890s.

The blazon

This blazon is an insignia that was awarded in the year of 1435. the owner of the blazon was Markgraf Friedrich 1 von Brandenburg and it marked the beginning of the nobels rule in parkstein.

Ceramic museum

The ceramic museum was build in April 1990 and exibits many relics of the old times. It has some ceramics that date back to the beginning of Germany.

a ceramic table

These tables were utilised for writing purposes, mostly for checks or keeping track of something important ,like heritage or marriage. This particular table depicts a "what-happens-if"-situation that happened about 1250 BC. in Syriea.

a winemixer

This vessel was called crater and it was used to mix wine with water. Due to the fact that drinking water was contaminated in the antique ,it was dangerouse the wine was used to disinfect it as it contained alcohol which killing moste of the bacteria present. This vessel was used in greece in 3 BC.

antique vase

This vase dates back to the 1200 century, it originates from china and was used to collect water and transported back on the head it also has an intricate design with handels formed after elephants and a picture painted in the middle.

practical uses

Ceramics had other uses besides the ones mentioned above for example: to make molds that were later used to make figures or toys. the mold was later destroyed to extract the casted item.

picture

This picture was made out of ceramic and shaped in a way that it presents a religiouse picture. If you look good enough in the down right part of this piece, you can still discern the authors signiture.
